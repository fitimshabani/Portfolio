About Me

Fitim Shabani

Back-end developer

I have always longed for a career in something creative combined with technical challenges. And therefore, after a few months of research, I introduced myself to the basics of programming languages like HTML , CSS and JavaScript, through the use of Codecademy and Youtube. Since being creative and methodical by nature I realised my interest grew in programming very fast and instantly chose to pursue my next career in Front-End Development.

In November 2017, few months after I had first approached the world of Web Development, I joined the 'Development Professional Programme' with Just IT. This self-funded course has been the first step in the journey towards becoming a front-end developer. Feeding my knowledge with web development languages and pushing my creative boundaries, my motivation and determination is at its highest and I look forward to learning more each day.

I had previously worked in the pharmaceutical industry for the last 8 years as a dispenser, involving numeric and scientific discipline,it was a very responsible role.
